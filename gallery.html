In the Hunza Valley region of northern Pakistan, the high mountain peaks of the Karakoram range appeal to trekkers and photographers. It’s one of many wild landscapes travelers can visit in this South Asian country.

Legends surround Saiful Muluk, a lake nestled in northern Pakistan’s Kaghan Valley at the western edge of the Himalaya.

The alpine plateaus of Pakistan’s Deosai National Park are populated by Tibetan wolves, Himalayan brown bears, and the grazing livestock of farmers.

The Baltoro Glacier covers 39 square miles in the eastern portion of the Karakoram range.
